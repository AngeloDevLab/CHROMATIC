# Elevator Pitch

Eons ago, a highly advanced civilization from another world created the Guardians: humanoid energy beings with power over color, built to push back an ancient, world-devouring Darkness. It could never be fully destroyed, only banished. Fearing the Darkness would reach other worlds as well, the civilization left hidden outposts scattered throughout them: silent anchor points, ready to release a Guardian should the need arise.

Now the Darkness reaches this world too. Just before it devours everything, you materialize on a remote beach in a color explosion and bring color back to a world that has fallen dark, with every step you take. Every enemy defeated, every region reclaimed brings a place back to life. Beneath the medieval surface of the world lie scattered remnants of an ancient network, and perhaps answers to who you really are.

CHROMATIC is a 2D action platformer about contrast: Darkness against color, forgotten high technology against a decayed present, and your duty against what you're still about to discover about yourself.

**Genre:** Sci-Fi Fantasy / 2D Action Platformer
